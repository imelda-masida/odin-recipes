salad

A great salad for everyone

With good spicies. yummm!

ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce leaves
  • 1 cucumber
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 carrot
  • 2 radishes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1½ tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: feta cheese, olives, boiled egg, nuts or seeds

steps

  1. Wash and chop vegetables
  2. Prepare the greens
  3. Make the dressing
  4. Combine
  5. Dress and serve
