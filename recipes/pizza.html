pizza

A great pizza for every family

home

ingredients

  • 2½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp active dry yeast
  • ½ cup warm water
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1½ tbsp olive oil
  • ⅓ tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp cornmeal (optional, for dusting)
  • 400g tomatoes (for sauce)
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp red chili flakes
  • 1 tsp Italian herbs
  • 200g mozzarella cheese
  • Mixed vegetables (onion, bell pepper, mushrooms, olives)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

steps

  1. Prepare the dough
  2. Make the sauce
  3. Prepare toppings
  4. Shape the dough
  5. Assemble the pizza
  6. Bake
